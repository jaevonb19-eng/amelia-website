Services

Secure Drive Sanitization

Drives are wiped using industry-standard methods appropriate for HDDs and SSDs, with verification and logging.

Asset Inventory & Tracking

Each system is logged with serial numbers and a unique internal asset ID. A manifest is created for your records.

No-Cost Pickup

We offer no-cost pickup of eligible decommissioned desktop computers for small businesses and offices.

Certificates of Data Destruction

After sanitization, documentation is provided showing the data destruction process performed.

Responsible Reuse & Recycling

Usable systems are refurbished for continued use. Non-usable components are recycled responsibly.

Our Process

  1. Equipment intake and inventory
  2. Drive sanitization using documented methods
  3. Verification and logging
  4. Certificate and asset manifest generation
  5. Reuse or responsible recycling

About
