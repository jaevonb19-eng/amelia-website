About Us

At Amelia Tech & Auto LLC, doing business as Amelia Asset Recovery, our mission is to help businesses, schools, and individuals securely dispose of old electronics while protecting sensitive data and supporting environmental sustainability. We follow best practices in IT asset management and ITAD standards to ensure complete accountability for every device collected.

Our trained staff handle pickups, logging, data wiping, and recycling. Every device is traceable in our secure inventory system, and documentation can be provided for compliance and auditing purposes. We are committed to maximizing reuse of electronics and minimizing e-waste.

Our Services

Free Pickup & Evaluation

We collect desktops, laptops, servers, monitors, printers, and peripherals from businesses, schools, and individuals at no cost. Devices are evaluated on-site or at our facility to determine if they can be reused, refurbished, or recycled.

Logged & Tracked Devices

Every device or storage medium is meticulously logged into our secure inventory system. This ensures full traceability from pickup to final processing, giving clients peace of mind that all equipment is accounted for.

ITAD Data Wipe & Security

We provide certified IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) data wiping services. Hard drives are securely erased using recognized methods (DoD 5220.22-M, NIST 800-88) or physically destroyed if requested. Documentation is available for every device, ensuring sensitive data is completely unrecoverable.

Responsible Recycling

Devices that cannot be reused or refurbished are recycled through certified e-waste partners. We follow a zero-landfill policy to ensure harmful materials are safely disposed of, reducing environmental impact.

Business Documentation

We provide detailed manifests, pickup logs, and data wipe certificates for businesses, schools, and organizations to support IT compliance, auditing, and reporting requirements.

How It Works

1. Request Pickup

Fill out our form or email us with device details and your location.

2. Schedule Collection

We coordinate a convenient pickup date and time that fits your schedule.

3. Device Logging

Each device is logged into our secure inventory system for accountability and traceability.

4. Data Wipe & ITAD

Drives are securely wiped or destroyed according to recognized ITAD standards, ensuring sensitive data is fully protected.

5. Refurbishment / Recycling

Usable devices are refurbished for reuse. Devices that cannot be reused are recycled responsibly through certified partners.

6. Documentation

Businesses and organizations receive pickup manifests and data wipe certificates for compliance and auditing purposes.
